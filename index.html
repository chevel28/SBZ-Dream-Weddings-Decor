About Us

The Visionaries Behind Your Dream Luxury Events

Welcome to SBZ Dream Weddings & Décor. We are passionate designers dedicated to turning your finest celebrations into majestic real-life moments. Based in Jamaica, our hallmark is bringing world-class elegance, pristine details, and sophisticated designs directly to your venue.

Whether crafting a romantic backdrop for an intimate exchange of vows, engineering a grand corporate gathering, or dressing up a breathtaking luxury wedding, we manage every layer of style. With a meticulous handle on lighting, custom backdrops, stages, and stunning floral features, our goal is simple: styling unforgettable surroundings and producing lasting memories.

Our Services

Bespoke Design, Production, and Full Event Styling

Wedding Production

From intimate beachside setups to magnificent ballroom transformations. We curate custom stage designs, gorgeous table settings, architectural fabrics, and romantic atmospheres matching your exact love story.

Bespoke Stage & Backdrops

Make a profound statement with high-end premium backdrops and luxury custom stages. Perfect for photo ops, sweetheart tables, key speakers, and center-stage production value.

Elite Décor & Lighting

Ambiance changes everything. We style events utilizing premium balloons, high-leverage ambient lighting arrays, structural floral setups, and luxury details engineered to shine bright.

Luxury Milestone Parties

Celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, and elite social gatherings in high style. We tailor custom thematic environments suited exclusively for your guest counts and distinct tastes.
